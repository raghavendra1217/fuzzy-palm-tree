The BIS Way

BIS endeavors to promote the all-round development of children. We are a close-knit family that focuses on giving individual attention to each student and making learning accessible to all. Our dedicated educators work closely with each child to support their unique needs and foster a love for learning.

With the right combination of universal and Indian values, we believe in helping every child grow into a responsible citizen and a good human being. We emphasize respect, empathy, and integrity as core principles in our teaching approach.

The Early Years at BIS is a family within a family. It caters to the specific needs of young learners in a safe and nurturing environment. Our early childhood programs are designed to spark curiosity and creativity, laying a solid foundation for future academic success.

Our commitment to education extends beyond academics. We offer a range of enrichment activities that support the emotional and social development of our students, ensuring they grow into well-rounded individuals.

Academics

Early Years: The Early Years at BIS is a family within a family. It caters to the specific needs of young learners in a safe and nurturing environment. The program focuses on building the foundation of learning with key skills in literacy and numeracy. Activities are designed to be engaging and age-appropriate, fostering a love for learning from an early age.

Elementary School: Our Elementary School provides a strong academic foundation with a balanced approach to learning and development. We focus on developing core skills in reading, writing, and mathematics, while also introducing students to the arts, sciences, and social studies.

Middle School: Middle School prepares students for the academic challenges of high school with a focus on critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Our curriculum includes a diverse range of subjects and projects that encourage analytical and creative thinking.

High School: High School offers a comprehensive curriculum to prepare students for higher education and future careers. We provide a range of elective courses and advanced placement options to help students pursue their interests and career goals.

Examinations: We offer a range of examinations to evaluate students' progress and achievements. These assessments help us tailor our teaching strategies to meet individual needs and ensure that students are well-prepared for their academic milestones.

Our academic programs are designed to foster a love for learning and prepare students for lifelong success. We encourage a growth mindset and provide support to help students achieve their personal and academic goals.

Admission Procedure

Admission to BIS is open to students of all nationalities who demonstrate the ability to benefit from our international curricula and programs. We seek students who are eager to learn and thrive in a diverse and dynamic educational environment.

Criteria for Admission:

  • Availability of seats in appropriate classes and programs
  • Meeting all admission procedures and qualifications
  • Meeting all financial obligations
